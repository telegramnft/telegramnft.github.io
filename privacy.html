Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data on the Telegram NFT website.

1. Information Collected

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. We only interact with your wallet address using TonConnect.

2. Telegram Login

If you use Telegram Login, we may receive your public Telegram name, username, and profile photo. This information is used only to personalize your experience.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics services. Your privacy is our priority.

4. Blockchain Transparency

All NFT transactions are recorded publicly on the TON blockchain. We cannot remove or hide any on-chain data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or trade any user data with third parties.

6. Changes

This privacy policy may be updated. Changes will be posted on this page.
